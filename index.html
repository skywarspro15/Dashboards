Features

  • Create, collaborate, and manage.

    Dashboards isn't just a dashboard. It could be your entire remote workplace. Dashboards integrates smoothly with your existing PowerUI services, such as Cloud, Chat, and Docs.

  • Real-time Collaboration

    Dashboards allow team members to collaborate with others in real-time. It also allows team members to communicate, with real-time text, voice, or video chat.

  • Safe & Secure

    All data transferred, and stored through Dashboards are encrypted. Logins are protected and secured with 2 factor authentication.

  • Fast & Reliable

    The Dashboards app is fast and reliable, hosted with reliable web hosts such as Netlify, we can ensure 99.9% uptime.

  • Completely Free & Open Source

    Getting started with Dashboards is free! Just sign up for a PowerUI account and you're set.
